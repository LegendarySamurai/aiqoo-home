About AIQHome TV

AIQHome 5 Star Magazine is the U.S number one source for everything you need to know about home improvement. Created to bring clarity to the foggy world of home renovation, AIQHome delivers fair, honest and reliable information, in article and video formats, to guide you effortlessly through the renovation process. AIQHome is tailored to different regions in the US so that you are provided with information that’s relevant to you wherever you are based and whichever project you are contemplating doing. AIQHome helps you avoid the common costly mistakes that occur during the renovation process, it can even help you locate the best suited contractor for your job.
