Roof costs vary by material, project size and obviously location (state, county even zip code).

Average national costs range between $5,000 and $20,000 for a 1,500 sq.ft. roof but can vary greatly.

So if you need to replace your roof and are looking for a detailed answer to how much would your roof cost, leave your details and professional roofing contractors operating in your area will provide you with a cost assessment of the project at hand.

All roofing contractors operating with AIQHome are required to hold active and applicable state licenses, carry insurance and all the accredited certifications enabling them to carry out roofing projects.

Please note that all licenses and insurance are time limited and must be periodically renewed. We strongly suggest that when speaking with prospective roofers you ask that they provide you with valid roofing licenses, applicable certifications and insurance to confirm their validity dates.

For additional information and tips on selecting the right contractor for your roofing project, check out our "How to find a reliable home contractor" clip on AIQHomeTV.

Of course. The PACE (Property Assessed Clean Energy) program is a financing initiative providing upfront costs for energy efficiency upgrades for homes including roof replacement HVAC systems, insulation and more.

The PACE program is currently available in a number of U.S states including California, Florida and Missouri and the list of available states for the program is growing.

Our top contractors specialize in the PACE program and are able to provide you with all the details and even get you qualified and approved with no hassle on your part at all.

If you want to connect with roof contractors who specialize in PACE financing tick the financing option when providing your details for your roof replacement project, and if you want to learn more about the PACE program click here for our PACE clip on AIQHomeTV.
