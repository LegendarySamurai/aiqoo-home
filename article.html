Meet the government backed program that will fund your new roof

PACE programs backed by the government recently have raised property values across the U.S and replaced its roofs in the process.

AiqHome
Editorial Team

Be sure to take notice of this incredible opportunity and use it. The PACE program supported by the government may pay for your new roof and even increase your property value.

A new roof - with Uncle Sam’s money

The government is now rewarding energy efficient measures through the PACE program. It stands for Property Assessed Clean Energy and the program applies to both sectors, C-PACE for commercial properties or R-PACE for residential properties.

Financing

Since 2017 over 4 billion dollars Was invested by american homeowners across the country in upgrades and improvements to their properties that will make them more energy efficient and qualify them for PACE financing, one of the more popular ways to improve your property before putting it on the market is to replace the roof- this is why this program has been already renowned as a success.

Why PACE?

There is No minimum Fico requirements and you can Prequalify in less than half an hour, that’s for starters. There’s No money down needed and the first year will be without any payments. Later on you can pay together with mortgage or property tax payments. The evaluation is equity based - (the owner must hold at least 10% equity stake) and it even Increases the chance to qualify for environmental tax credits.

An increase in your Property value

Experts in Real estate agree that replacing your roof has a terrific ROI (return on investment) and can sometimes increase the value by more than the cost, and basically pays itself off.

While interior design gets the attention- It’s the roof replacement that will be the key factor in reducing your maintenance cost.

There are many more reasons for having your roof replaced, maintenance costs can be dramatically reduced when preventing leaks to cause thousands of dollars in damage to dry walls or floors. Energy costs might be reduced by as much as 20%, while you actually contribute to the fight against climate change and pollution, Not mentioning the fact that you will be getting a new look that will help give your home an elevated style and value.

To sum things up

It's obvious why so many Americans are taking advantage of this government backed program and getting new roofs. Replacing your roof will lower your utilities and maintenance costs and we raise your property's value and appeal.
