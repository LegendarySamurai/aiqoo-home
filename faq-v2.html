Social distancing measures are recommended during the pandemic; however home repair and renovation projects should not be neglected. Following the guidelines provided by the federal and state advisors will greatly assist you in keeping safe: Maintain the 6-foot separation recommendation during their visit. Ask that the service provider and their team wear protective masks (and possibly gloves) during their full stay in your home and for your safety, consider wearing a mask yourself as well. Finally, sanitize and disinfect all work areas and frequently touched fixtures (door knobs, light switches etc.) after the visit.
